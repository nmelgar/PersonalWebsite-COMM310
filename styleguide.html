Overview

Site Purpose

This website is to show the different digital compositions; icons, logos and other art created by me. It will also show web pages created by me and other important stuff about technology and art.

Target Audience

It is to people from 16 to 60 years old, both men or women, since it is more about art and technology it will be interesting for both.
It could be general, but I want my website to be visited by all kind of persons around the worlds, they would need to understand English or Spanish language to better understand what's the content about.
They could be married or single, searching for interesting compositions to use on their websites, searching for inspiration or just to have a good time observing my compositions.
They may be studied or not for the art, art is for every different person of the world with or without education, maybe for the the web pages this could be different because they will need more understanding about the subject to better appreciate those creations.

Color Palette

I like these colors, learning about color psychology that they have a special. Blue involves creativity, safety and serenity, these is something people should seek while navigating on internet and watching art.
Green is more about balance, renewal and growth, every time we learn more about art, programming or the stuff shared on my website we are growing in our knowledge towards becoming better persons.
