What processs do I follow?

1. I look around for inspiration: This may include watching
a movie, cartoon, videogame or other artist's compositions.
2. Sketch on my mind what I'm going to create, colors, characteristics
and other details, always looking for the final design.
3. Sketch on paper how the character will look like.

4. Start the digital sketch with minor details
5. This may be a step for a beginner like me, but I try to imitate
professional artist's details on their creations while doing mines.
6. Detail as much as possible
7. Add shadows and colors if necessary.
8. Final details and export the creation.
